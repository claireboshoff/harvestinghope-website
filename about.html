About Harvesting Hope

Our Mission. Our Team. Our Vision.

We believe no family should go hungry when the land beneath their feet can provide.

Our Story

Born from a Simple Truth

Harvesting Hope started with a question: if African soil can grow food, why are millions going hungry? The answer wasn't the land — it was access. Access to quality seeds. Access to water-efficient systems. Access to soil that hasn't been depleted.

Founded by Carin Moffett, Harvesting Hope set out to solve all three problems in a single kit. Today, every kit we deliver gives a family the tools to feed themselves — not for one meal, but for every season that follows.

Our kits have reached 50 communities across 5 countries, and every delivery proves the same thing: when you give people the right tools, they don't just survive. They thrive.
