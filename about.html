About Harvesting Hope

Mission & Vision

Rooted in Faith,
Growing with Purpose

At the core of Harvesting Hope, our mission is twofold. We aim to revolutionize sustainable agriculture across Africa, turning barren plots into thriving gardens with our clever watering system, premium seeds, and natural bio-fertilizer. Simultaneously, we are committed to spreading God's word, believing in the power of faith to uplift and inspire communities toward a brighter, hope-filled future.

Our vision is a continent where no family goes hungry, where communities are self-sufficient, and where faith and agriculture work hand in hand to create lasting transformation.

A Seed of Inspiration

Harvesting Hope was born from the vision of Carin Moffett, who saw the vast potential of Africa's barren land and the resilience of its people. Where others saw only drought and hardship, Carin saw opportunity — opportunity to transform empty plots into flourishing gardens that could feed families and restore dignity.

Driven by a deep faith and an unwavering belief in community, Carin assembled a team of passionate innovators, each bringing unique expertise to the table. Together, they developed an approach that combines premium seeds, an ingenious bucket watering system, and natural bio-fertiliser to make sustainable agriculture accessible to every family.

Today, Harvesting Hope continues to grow, reaching new communities and proving that with the right tools, the right knowledge, and a little faith, anything is possible.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision we make and every seed we plant.

🌎

Sustainability

We develop agricultural solutions that work with nature, not against it. Every product and method we create is designed for long-term ecological balance and self-sufficiency.

☤

Faith

Our work is rooted in the belief that God's word and His love can uplift and inspire communities. Faith is the foundation upon which we build hope and resilience.

🤝

Community

We believe in the strength of togetherness. By empowering communities to grow together, we create networks of support that endure long after the first harvest.

💪

Empowerment

We do not just provide food — we provide the tools and knowledge for families to feed themselves. True change comes when people are equipped to shape their own futures.

💡

Innovation

From our clever bucket watering system to our specially selected seed varieties, we constantly seek smarter, more effective ways to make agriculture accessible and productive.
