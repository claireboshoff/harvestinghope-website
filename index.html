The Problem

Hunger in South Africa Is a Solvable Problem

13.5 Million Go Hungry

Over 13.5 million South Africans experience food insecurity — not from lack of land, but from lack of tools and knowledge.

Drought Kills Crops

Traditional seeds and watering methods fail in South Africa's increasingly erratic rainfall patterns. Communities lose entire seasons.

Depleted Soil

Decades of poor farming practices have stripped the soil of the nutrients needed to sustain crops — even when water is available.

The Solution

Everything a Family Needs in One Kit

Each R1,500 kit is a complete food-growing system — three components that work together to turn any plot of land into a productive family garden.

Component 1

Premium Seed Kit

Drought-resistant, high-yield varieties optimised for African climates — spinach, Swiss chard, moringa, tomatoes, onions, garlic, amaranth, cowpeas, and herbs.

Component 2

Bucket Watering System

Gravity-fed drip irrigation that uses 70% less water than traditional methods. No electricity required. Built from locally available materials for easy repairs.

Component 3

Bio-Fertiliser

Organic microbe-rich soil enhancer that rebuilds depleted farmland using beneficial bacteria and fungi. No chemicals. Self-sustaining after the first season.

How It Works

From Donation to Harvest in 90 Days

1

You Donate a Kit

Your R1,500 donation covers one complete kit for a family in need. We handle sourcing, packing, and delivery — 100% of funds go directly to kits.

2

We Deliver & Train

Our field team delivers the kit and provides hands-on planting guidance. No prior farming experience needed.

3

The Family Plants

Using the seeds, drip system, and bio-fertiliser together, families establish their garden using techniques that suit their local conditions.

4

They Harvest & Replant

Within 90 days, families are harvesting food. Crops generate seeds for the next season — creating a self-sustaining food cycle.
